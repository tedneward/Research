title=OASIS Advanced Message Queue Protocol (AMQP)
tags=distribution, format, messaging, specification
summary=The global standard enterprise messaging protocol.
~~~~~~

Initiated by JPMorgan, it's the standard protocol in many areas of the financial industry, trade, and industrial applications. It has been developed in OASIS and standardized by ISO as 19464:2014.

AMQP is implemented by a most major queue-oriented message brokers.

* [AMQP 1.0](https://docs.oasis-open.org/amqp/core/v1.0/os/amqp-core-overview-v1.0-os.html) core standard document. Also ISO 19464:2014.

  * [AMQP Filter Expressions Version 1.0](https://docs.oasis-open.org/amqp/filtex/v1.0/csd01/filtex-v1.0-csd01.html)
