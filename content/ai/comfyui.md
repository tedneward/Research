## Resources

- A Simple Implementation of Qwen3-TTS's ComfyUI [Source](https://github.com/flybirdxx/ComfyUI-Qwen-TTS): ComfyUI custom nodes for speech synthesis, voice cloning, and voice design, based on the open-source Qwen3-TTS project by the Alibaba Qwen team.
- Qwen3 Audiobook Converter [Source](https://github.com/WhiskeyCoder/Qwen3-Audiobook-Converter): Convert PDFs, EPUBs, DOCX, DOC, and TXT files into high-quality audiobooks using Qwen3 TTS Voice Model - an open-source voice synthesis system that excels at natural speech generation and voice cloning.
- Qwen3-TTS text-to-speech nodes for ComfyUI with voice cloning, voice design, and fine-tuning UI [Source](https://github.com/filliptm/ComfyUI-FL-Qwen3TTS): Advanced text-to-speech nodes for ComfyUI powered by Alibaba's Qwen3-TTS model family. Features voice cloning, voice design from text descriptions, predefined speakers, and a built-in fine-tuning UI with real-time training dashboard.
