[LM Link](https://lmstudio.ai/link): Load models on remote machines and use them as if they are local. End-to-end encrypted. Works for local devices, LLM rigs, or cloud VMs.

## Installation

### Headless

Introducing `llmster`. It's LM Studio's core, but without the GUI. Deploy on Linux boxes, cloud servers, or even in CI.

Mac / Linux: `curl -fsSL https://lmstudio.ai/install.sh | bash`

Windows: `irm https://lmstudio.ai/install.ps1 | iex`
