title=Franc
tags=library, nodejs
summary=Natural language detection--infer the language from the text passed.
~~~~~~

Detect the language of text. franc is packaged with support for 82, 187, or 406 languages and has a CLI.

[Github](https://github.com/wooorm/franc)

Franc is a derivative work from [guess-language](https://github.com/kent37/guess-language) (Python, LGPL), [guesslanguage](http://websvn.kde.org/branches/work/sonnet-refactoring/common/nlp/guesslanguage.cpp?view=markup) (C++, LGPL), and [Language::Guess](http://web.archive.org/web/20090228163219/http://languid.cantbedone.org/) (Perl, GPL). Franc has been ported to several other programming languages: Elixir — [paasaa](https://github.com/minibikini/paasaa), Erlang — [efranc](https://github.com/G-Corp/efranc), Go — [franco](https://github.com/kapsteur/franco), [whatlanggo](https://github.com/abadojack/whatlanggo), R — [franc](https://github.com/MangoTheCat/franc), Rust — [whatlang-rs](https://github.com/greyblake/whatlang-rs)
