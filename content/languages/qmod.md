title=Qmod
tags=language, quantum
summary=A quantum modeling language developed by Classiq that operates at a higher level of abstraction than gate-level frameworks like Qiskit or Cirq.
~~~~~~

[Website](https://docs.classiq.io/latest/qmod-reference/)

Rather than specifying individual gates and qubit operations, a developer describes the functional intent of an algorithm in Qmod and the Classiq synthesis engine generates an optimized, hardware-aware circuit automatically. Qmod also supports both a native syntax and a Python SDK embedding, making it accessible to developers already working in Python. The language integrates with IBM, IonQ, AWS Braket, Azure Quantum, and [NVIDIA](https://app.thequantuminsider.com/investor/2c6d1edb-94d8-4081-993b-6675137e3738/profile) backends.
