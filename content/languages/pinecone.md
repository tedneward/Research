summary=Pinecone is a brand new, easy to learn, general purpose, multi-paradigm, high performance programming language created by Sophie Winter. Work on the language began on October 4th, 2016. Pinecone can now be interpreted or transpiled to C++. The language is written from scratch (it includes an integrated lexer, parser and interpreter, etc.).
