title=XIDEK

summary=Technical guidance and source code so that you can readily design and implement an interpreter according to your own requirements.
~~~~~~

[Source](http://www.parsifalsoft.com/examples/xidek/index.html)

You may need, for example, to create a special interpreter for a domain specific language, script language, or other "little language". XIDEK can save you weeks or months of work. It gives you parsers, support modules, explanations, examples, organization and a framework upon which you can build.
